# Entry Checklist

1. Higher Timeframe Bias: Confirm direction on daily/4H.
2. Liquidity Sweep: Did price sweep recent high/low liquidity?
3. Displacement: Strong move after sweep?
4. Market Structure Shift (MSS): Break of structure confirming bias?
5. Fair Value Gap (FVG) or Pullback: Price retracing to FVG or discount/premium zone?
6. Stop‑Loss Placement: Based on invalidation point (e.g., opposite OB, swing point).
7. Take‑Profit Plan: Target based on opposite liquidity, equity swing, or risk‑reward.
8. Risk‑Reward Ratio: Minimum 1:2 (or as per plan).
9. Emotional Check: Are you entering due to FOMO, frustration, or revenge?
10. Additional Confirmation: Any confluences (order block, higher‑timeframe OB, session open, etc.)?
