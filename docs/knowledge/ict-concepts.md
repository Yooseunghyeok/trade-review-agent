# ICT Concepts Quick Reference

## Liquidity Sweep
Price moves to grab stop‑loss orders or resting liquidity before reversing.

## Market Structure Shift (MSS)
A break of the most recent swing high/low that indicates a change in trend direction.

## Fair Value Gap (FVG)
An imbalance where price leaves a gap; often acts as magnet for future retracement.

## Displacement
Strong, rapid price move that creates imbalance and often precedes a sweep or FVG.

## Premium / Discount
Premarket: price above fair value (overbought). Discount: price below fair value (oversold) relative to ICT equilibrium.

## Order Block
The last opposing candle before a strong move; acts as support/resistance where institutions may have entered.

## Higher Timeframe Bias
The dominant trend direction on a higher timeframe (e.g., daily) that lower‑timeframe trades should align with.
